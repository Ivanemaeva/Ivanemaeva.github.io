Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This website is the personal portfolio of Ivane Maeva Nague. It respects your privacy — here is exactly what happens with your data, in plain language.

What data is collected

The only data collected is what you voluntarily submit through the contact form:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your email address
  • The subject and content of your message

No other personal data is collected. This site uses no cookies, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind.

Why it is collected

Solely to read and respond to your enquiry. Nothing else.

How it is processed

The contact form is operated by Formspree, a form-processing service that transmits your message to my email inbox. Formspree processes your submission in accordance with its own privacy policy.

How long it is kept

Your message stays in my email inbox for as long as needed to handle our exchange. It is never sold, shared, or used for marketing.

Your rights

Under the GDPR, you can ask at any time to access, correct, or delete the data you submitted. Just email naguemaeva26@gmail.com and it will be done.

Hosting

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect technical logs (such as IP addresses) as part of operating the hosting service, as described in its privacy statement.
